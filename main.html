Filling Analysis 1

In the given video Filling analysis of AFC/ SFC Fluid Coupling is shown by simulation in the Altair Inspire Cast Software. The material used for casting is Aluminum LM6 metal. The given coupling casting having diameter 417 mm, since it is big casting. Weight of casting is 8.5 kg.

In this case this analysis gives the idea about where does metal solidifies earlier, it gives mis run or cold shut defect information. We also get know that how does the molten metal flows in the casting, and overview of how mould is filling with molten metal. We also get velocity condition in different region so we can modify the cross section or dimensions of gate, runner, etc.

Temperature Analysis 1

In the given video Temperature analysis of AFC/ SFC Fluid Coupling is shown by simulation in the Altair Inspire Cast Software. The given coupling casting having diameter 417 mm. The material used for casting is Aluminum LM6 metal. Weight of casting is 8.5 kg.

In the given casting cold shut has been observed, it is also refered as mis-run. Especially cold shut are seen in ribs. Basically cold shuts are caused when molten metal flow comes into contact with cooler/cold surface and solidifies before complete filling of the mould.By using this analysis, we get information about how does the cooling of molten metal takes place. The regions we get more temperature, and where it cools rapidly. Hence can easily detect porosity, blowholes.
